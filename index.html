Rules

  • The game has two players playing in turns

  • In each turn player rolls a dice, as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to player's CURRENT score and player's turn continues

  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing, they lose all their CURRENT score and it becomes the next player's turn

  • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their GLOBAL score, and it becomes the next player's turn

  • The first player to score 100 or more points wins the game
